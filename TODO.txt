Wave Survival
-------------

[仇] Skeletons don't attack but instead flicker their bows up and down

[仇] Piglins don't attack move for some reason?

[仇] Default the gamemode selector to survival

[游리] You now lose when all buildings are destroyed instead of just your capitol, just like in multiplayer

[九] Raised quantity of spawns for waves 3+

[九] Skeletons can now spawn on the first wave

[游리] Improved spawn location logic again
    - This time, enemies only try to spawn near your outermost buildings to avoid a chance of a wave failing to spawn
    - If none or less than the required enemies spawned, a warning is sent to all players
        - If you see this warning, please tell me and show me a screenshot of your base and map

[九] Waves now go up to 30, maxing out at 700 total population worth of enemies
    - The spawn logic is likely going to bug out by this point and not actually spawn that many units
    - (I wasn't able to test this properly because I was afraid of melting my PC)

[游리] Mob frostwalker now works on lava by turning the lava into magma

[游리] Added Illager waves
    - Illagers spawn in one large group

[游리] Added Piglin waves
    - Piglins spawn from portals which pop up at the start of the night
    - Half of the wave's population is spawned immediately
    - Portals will trickle enemies in forever until destroyed
    - Currently cannot spawn on water or lava


Features
--------

Villager Militia
    - Town Centres and villagers now have a 'Call to Arms' button
    - When used, they run to the nearest Town Centre to be turned into a Militia unit
    - Militia have 35 HP and 3 damage but cannot do regular worker activities
    - Militia transform back into villagers automatically after 60 seconds
    - Added a bell to the town centre
    [仇] Return to work button to automatically return to last known gathering source/position
        - save last known position on the ability
    [仇] Town bell stops moving halfway

Bugfixes
--------

[游리] Portals can sometimes stack the same upgrade if they're upgraded with multiple selected

[游리] Illagers can no longer spawn with random enchantments on their equipment

[游리] Ranged units now stagger their attack speeds by a few ticks randomly
    - This is to work around Minecraft's built-in 1-tick invincibility frames

[仇] Teams sometimes switch for some reason
    - only happens in wave survival?

[仇] Can't control units when they're on slabs?

[游리] Animals cannot spawn on Y levels too different to your capitol

[游리] Queued units no longer progress if you're at the population cap (instead of being stuck at 99%)
    - This is to fix an issue of being able to go over the max cap when the server is lagging
